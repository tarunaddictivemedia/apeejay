Apeejay School International: Premier IB School in South Delhi

50+ Years Of Education Excellence

Imagine a school where your child's unique potential is nurtured in a world-class environment. Apeejay School International (ASI), is a premier IB day boarding school in a prime location in South Delhi.

As Delhi's first IB day boarding school, ASI proudly offers the complete International Baccalaureate (IB) continuum, providing a seamless educational journey from primary years through to the diploma program. Our state-of-the-art, centrally air-conditioned campus boasts exceptional facilities, including modern resource centers, art studios, swimming pools, a robotics lab, and high-tech classrooms.

At ASI, we go beyond traditional education. Our approach combines inquiry-based learning, personalized attention, and holistic development, preparing students to solve and manage real-life problems. With a focus on cultivating global citizens, we empower our students to think critically, act compassionately, and adapt confidently in an ever-changing world. Join us for a campus tour and discover how ASI can unlock your child's full potential. Contact us today to schedule your visit and take the first step towards an extraordinary educational journey.

World-Class Amenities

World-Class Amenities, Unmatched Comfort.

Art Studio

A dynamic space designed to spark creativity. With top-notch materials, including paints, brushes, and digital art tools, students can explore various artistic mediums. The studio’s spacious work areas, professional lighting, and expert instructors provide an ideal environment for artistic growth. Here, every student’s imagination is nurtured, allowing their artistic visions to come to life.

Dance Studio

Our Dance Studio is a hub of energy and expression. Designed to inspire movement, it features professional-grade flooring, mirrors, and sound systems to support a range of dance styles. The spacious studio accommodates both individual practice and group choreography, while our experienced instructors guide students in developing their technique and performance skills.

Dining Hall

Our Dining Hall is more than just a place to eat—it's a vibrant social hub. With its modern design and spacious layout, the hall provides a comfortable setting for students to enjoy their meals. The menu features a variety of options, catering to diverse tastes and dietary needs. It's a place where students can gather, share moments, and fuel their day in a welcoming and lively environment.

Swimming Pool

The school has a 13 mtr x 2.50 mtr sized Swimming Pool having a water filter plant, and proper changing rooms. Students can participate in various aquatic programs, from swim clubs to synchronized swimming, enriching their extracurricular options.

Music Studio

Our Music Studio is a creative heaven for aspiring musicians. Equipped with high-quality instruments, soundproof rooms, and advanced recording technology. Whether practicing solo or in groups, students benefit from personalized guidance from skilled instructors.

Sports

At Apeejay, qualities like leadership and sportsmanship are closely interwoven in the School’s curriculum.Cricket, Football, Basketball, Throw ball, Volleyball are some of the games played. Special coaching in various sports events is arranged.
